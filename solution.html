Our approach

Our solution

6ix Nations is an offline-first education and transparency platform designed to address poverty and corruption in the DRC. It provides offline skills training in agriculture, technical trades, and entrepreneurship; connects users to jobs and local economic pathways; and improves transparency in natural-resource revenue management by letting citizens anonymously and safely report corruption, unpaid wages, and failed projects.

Fit

Problem → solution fit

Features

What the platform offers

Offline course library

Downloadable lessons covering practical vocational knowledge and small-business skills.

Job matching

Connects learners to jobs, apprenticeships, and local cooperative opportunities.

Anonymous corruption reporting

Offers safe, confidential submission channels for allegations and grievances.

Revenue transparency dashboard

Shows how reported resource revenues are allocated across public priorities.

Trusted Community Messenger network

Uses community champions to broadcast verified information and reinforce trust.

Local-language voice reporting

Supports voice-based reporting for people without reliable text access or smartphones.
