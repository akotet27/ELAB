Why we exist

Our mission

Our mission is to equip Congolese citizens with practical skills and transparent access to resource-revenue information, so that the DRC's mineral wealth translates into jobs, accountability, and improved living standards by 2035.

Looking to 2035

Our vision

A Democratic Republic of Congo where every citizen — regardless of location or connectivity — has the skills to earn a livelihood and the tools to hold power accountable for the nation's resource wealth.

What guides us

Core values

Transparency

Citizens have a right to see how resource revenue is managed and spent — not just told it is.

Inclusion

Our tools work offline and in local languages, so no community is left behind by connectivity or literacy.

Accountability

Citizen reports connect directly to oversight and follow-up, not just a database that goes unread.

Opportunity

Practical skills training turns knowledge into income that's independent of the mining economy.

By 2035

What success looks like

Lower underemployment

Trained users matched to real jobs and cooperatives, cutting into the 73% underemployment rate we set out to address.

Higher reporting, higher resolution

More citizens reporting corruption and unpaid wages, with visible follow-through on the public revenue dashboard.

Reach into rural communities

An offline course library and voice-based reporting available nationwide, not just where internet access already exists.
